HURRICARS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Hurricars, we collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Location data: city, state, ZIP code (USA only)
  • Vehicle listing content: photos, descriptions, pricing
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe (we never store card details)
  • Usage data: app interactions, search queries, viewed listings
  • Device information: device type, OS version, push notification token

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To publish and moderate vehicle listings
  • To process payments for top placement auctions via Stripe
  • To send push notifications about messages, bids, and listing status
  • To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • To comply with applicable US laws and regulations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Firebase / Google — cloud infrastructure and analytics
  • Law enforcement — when required by law or to prevent fraud

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to: know what personal data is collected, request deletion of their data, opt out of sale of personal data (we do not sell data), and non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@hurricars.app

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security including Firebase Security Rules, TLS encryption, and Stripe's PCI-compliant payment processing.

7. Children's Privacy

Hurricars is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@hurricars.app
